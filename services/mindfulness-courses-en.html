Mindfulness Courses

Let's get to know ourselves better.

Mindfulness is the ability to look at the unfolding of our experience moment by moment, in a non-judgmental way. By practicing it regularly throughout our day, we can reduce many issues (depression, anxiety/stress, chronic pain, blood pressure, etc.) and increase our abilities (concentration, memory, sleep, immune system, etc.). But don't take our word for it, look at what the American Mindfulness Research Association (AMRA) as to say.
